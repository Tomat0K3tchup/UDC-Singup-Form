Refund Policy

Courses

Should a student fail to complete their course for any reason, charges shall be levied as follows:

  • After reservation only: the $200 deposit will not be refunded.
  • After 1 day: $150 + $3 reef fee + $90 materials and 1 free night at the hotel.
  • After 2 days if no boat/diving trip has been taken: $180 + $6 reef fee + $90 materials and 2 free nights at the hotel.
  • After 2 days if the student has been on a boat/diving trip, or after 3 days regardless: no refund will be offered.

Private Room Upgrade

Should a student with a private room upgrade fail to complete his/her course (open water, advanced, rescue) and get reimbursed in any amount, the room upgrade discount will be cancelled, and regular room rates will apply.

Fun dives

For any cancellations for guided dives, the office must be notified before 5:00 PM on the day prior to the dive trip. Any last-minute cancellations are lost reservations and non-refundable. Free fun dives are offered as part of core course bookings (OW, AOW, Rescue) and are only valid until the day after the course has been completed and are not transferable.
